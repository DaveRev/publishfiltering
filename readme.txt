Publish Filtering
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Version: 1.004
Author: Nick Dunn <nick.dunn@airlock.com> (Originally by Rowan Lewis <rowan@pixelcarnage.com>)
Build Date: 2009-05-01
Requirements: Symphony 2.0.1


Installation
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Upload the 'publishfiltering' folder in this archive to your Symphony
   'extensions' folder.

2. Enable it by selecting the "Publish Filtering", choose Enable from the
   with-selected menu, then click Apply.

3. Publish index pages now have a form that lets you filter the entries.
